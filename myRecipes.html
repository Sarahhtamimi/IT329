Matcha Rose Latte
  • Matcha powder — 1½ tsp
  • Warm water — 60 ml
  • Milk (or oat milk) — 1 cup
  • Rose water — ½ tsp
  • Honey — 1 tsp
  • Pistachio crumbs — 1 tbsp
  1. Sift matcha into a cup and add warm water.
  2. Whisk until silky and foam forms.
  3. Warm the milk and froth it until airy.
  4. Stir rose water and honey into the milk.
  5. Pour milk over matcha, then top with pistachio crumbs.

Date & Almond Yogurt Bowl
  • Greek yogurt — 1 cup
  • Medjool dates — 3, chopped
  • Almonds — 2 tbsp
  • Chia seeds — 1 tsp
  • Vanilla — ½ tsp
  • Chai cinnamon — pinch
  1. Mix yogurt with vanilla and a pinch of cinnamon.
  2. Add chopped dates and stir gently.
  3. Top with almonds and chia seeds.
  4. Let it sit 5 minutes for texture.
  5. Serve chilled and glossy.

Salmon with Qahwa Spice Rub
  • Salmon fillet — 2 pieces
  • Olive oil — 1 tbsp
  • Qahwa spice mix — 1 tsp
  • Lemon zest — ½ tsp
  • Sea salt — to taste
  • Fresh herbs — handful
  1. Pat salmon dry and brush with olive oil.
  2. Rub with qahwa spices, salt, and lemon zest.
  3. Grill 3–4 minutes per side until flaky.
  4. Rest for 2 minutes to keep it juicy.
  5. Finish with herbs and a squeeze of lemon.
